Destinations

Racha Yai

Ideal for beginners. Experienced divers come here only to get in shape after long breaks between dives. The main advantages of Koh Racha Yai are: 20 km from Phuket, a shallow sandy bottom; clear, calm water and good visibility under water throughout the year.

READ MORE

Anemone Reef

A limestone rock, which rises from a depth of 30 meters, its eastern slope is a gentle descent to the sandy bottom, the western is a sheer wall. The abundance of anemones and nutrient-rich water attract an incredible amount of tropical fish to Anemone Reef.

READ MORE

King Cruiser

King Cruiser is a large car ferry that sank in 1997 after a collision with the underwater part of Anemone Reef and turned into an artificial coral reef. Only certified divers can dive at King Cruiser. Perfect for those who like wreck diving and enjoy the mysteries of the sank ships.

Scuba Diving Course

If you have already tried scuba diving, and you liked it, and you want to know more, be more independent under the water and dive in the places for certified divers, then the Open Water Diver diving course in Phuket is where you need to start. The course is divided into several parts: theory, practice, and of course, diving itself. It's simple: you go through all these parts of training in parallel: theory and practice in the pool or shallow water, and then real dives in the sea. During the training, you will learn everything that a diver should know: how the equipment works and how to use it and take care of it; where and when you can dive and how to plan your dives, how to swim under the water. You will learn so much that every day will bring you more and more vivid impressions!

Courses

PADI Open Water Course

A 3-4 days course for beginners. Students are introduced to the equipment, the basics of scuba diving, various techniques, and receive a certificate.

PADI Advanced Open Water Course

The course consists of 5 dives, 2 of which are mandatory - a deep sea dive (no deeper than 30m) and navigation. The 3 dives are left to the diver's discretion.

PADI Rescue Diver Course

Learning the basics of saving a diver, the skills to overcome and prevent problems both on the surface and under water. The course lasts for 2 days.

PADI Divemaster Course

The course consists of an extensive theoretical part, and also includes assistance to the instructor in the pool and in open water. PADI AOWD needed.
